Yucheng Li

I am a PhD student at the University of Surrey, co-affiliated at the NLP group in the University of Manchester, supervised by Dr. Frank Guerin and Prof. Chenghua Lin. Before starting my PhD in 2021, I obtained my MSc degree at East China Normal University and BSc degree at Shanghai Normal University.

My research mainly focuses on efficient LLMs Inference, Long Context LLMs, LLMs evaluation, and metaphor processing.

I am currently a Research Intern at Microsoft Research mentored by Huiqiang Jiang.
